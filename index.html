About

Tattooing since 2007. International artist working across Canada, the UK, and Australia. Specializing in large-scale illustrative realism, black & grey portraiture, and complex cover-ups.

Glen Hartless began tattooing in the winter of 2007 and has built an international career defined by discipline, adaptability, and technical precision. His work is structured to hold integrity over time, with a focus on contrast, composition, and long-term readability.

Having lived and tattooed around the world — from Australia to the United Kingdom — Glen has worked at dozens of international conventions, refining a globally influenced and performance-driven approach to tattooing.

A Pro Team Artist sponsored by EMALLA, Glen works with industry-leading equipment to maintain consistency and technical excellence across every project.

Throughout his career, he has tattooed public figures and recording artists including Justin Bieber, Chris Brown, 21 Savage, Drake, Christie Mack, Angelina Jolie, and many others. He has tattooed live on MTV and MuchMusic, and once completed a tattoo mid-flight at 10,000 feet.

Glen is known for taking on technically demanding projects, particularly large-scale compositions and complex cover-ups, approaching each piece with intensity and control.

How It Works

  1. Send your idea with placement, approximate size, and reference images.
  2. Your project is reviewed to make sure it fits the style, scale, and schedule.
  3. A deposit is required to secure your appointment.

What I Take On

Large-scale custom tattoos, illustrative realism, black & grey work, portraits, full sleeves, back pieces, chest pieces, and complex cover-ups.

What I Do Not Take On

Small walk-ins, copied Pinterest designs, rushed projects, or work that does not fit the style and quality standard of the studio.

Pricing

Projects are priced based on size, placement, detail, and complexity. Full-day sessions are preferred for larger work.
