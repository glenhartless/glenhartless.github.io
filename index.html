About

Glen Hartless is an international illustrative realism tattoo artist with 17 years of professional experience. His work emphasizes depth, structure, and long-term integrity in black and grey portraiture and large-scale compositions.

Having lived and worked in Australia and the United Kingdom, Glen has tattooed at dozens of international conventions, refining a disciplined and globally influenced approach to realism.

He is a Pro Team Artist for EMALLA, utilizing industry-leading equipment to maintain precision and consistency in every project.
